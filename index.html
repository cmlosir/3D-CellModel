1. The Animal Cell (Eukaryotic)

An animal cell is a type of eukaryotic cell that lacks a cell wall. Notice the flexible membrane and the variety of organelles like the Mitochondria and Nucleus.

Model: Eukaryotic Cell by SGU MedArt

2. The Plant Cell

Plant cells are also eukaryotic but distinct from animal cells. Look for: The rigid outer Cell Wall (which gives it a fixed shape) and the green Chloroplasts used for photosynthesis.
